About Kolam AI

Kolam AI is a project that combines traditional Tamil Kolam art with modern artificial intelligence. The application can classify different types of Kolam patterns and generate new patterns based on traditional designs.

Kolam is a form of drawing that is practiced in the Indian subcontinent, particularly in Tamil Nadu. It is a geometrical line drawing composed of curved loops, drawn around a grid pattern of dots.

Features

  • Classify Kolam images into different categories
  • Generate new Kolam patterns based on traditional designs
  • Learn about the cultural significance of different Kolam patterns

Cultural Significance

Kolams are thought to bring prosperity to homes and are drawn every morning in many South Indian homes. They are also drawn during festivals and special occasions.
